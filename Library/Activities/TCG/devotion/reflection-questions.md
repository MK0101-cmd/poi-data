# Reflection Questions - Devotion

## Core Self-Assessment Questions

### Understanding Devotion
- **What is the difference between commitment and devotion?**
  - *Purpose*: Explore personal understanding of devotion vs. commitment
  - *Follow-up*: How do you distinguish between the two in your own life?

- **Just how truly committed to myself, my relationship, my parenthood, my work, my life am I?**
  - *Purpose*: Assess current level of devotion in different life areas
  - *Follow-up*: Where do you feel most devoted? Least devoted?

- **What are the limitations of commitment?**
  - *Purpose*: Examine what prevents full devotion
  - *Follow-up*: What would need to change to increase devotion?

- **Am I willing to pay the price of my devotion?**
  - *Purpose*: Explore willingness to sacrifice for what matters
  - *Follow-up*: What price would you be willing to pay?

## Deeper Exploration Questions

### Personal Devotion
- **What am I most devoted to in my life?**
- **What would I be willing to sacrifice for?**
- **How do I know when I'm truly devoted vs. just committed?**

### Barriers to Devotion
- **What prevents me from being fully devoted?**
- **What fears or concerns hold me back from complete commitment?**
- **How do I handle the uncertainty of full devotion?**

### Devotion and Values
- **How does my devotion align with my core values?**
- **What would I be devoted to if I couldn't fail?**
- **How can I deepen my devotion to what matters most?**

## Group Discussion Questions

### Collective Devotion
- **How do we as a group or team demonstrate devotion?**
- **What role do I play in supporting others' devotion?**
- **How can we create a culture of devotion?**

### Devotion and Relationships
- **How does devotion affect my relationships?**
- **What does it mean to be devoted to another person?**
- **How can I support others in their devotion?**

## Action-Oriented Questions

### Immediate Application
- **What's one area where I could increase my devotion?**
- **What's one thing I could stop doing to focus on what matters?**
- **How could I demonstrate my devotion more clearly?**

### Long-term Development
- **What would my life look like if I were fully devoted?**
- **How can I develop greater capacity for devotion?**
- **What support do I need to maintain my devotion?**

## Facilitation Notes

### For Trainers
- **Timing**: Allow 20-25 minutes for individual reflection
- **Environment**: Create a safe space for honest self-assessment
- **Follow-up**: Use responses to identify devotion patterns and barriers

### Common Responses to Watch For
- **Fear**: "I'm afraid of being too devoted"
- **Confusion**: "I don't know what I should be devoted to"
- **Overwhelm**: "I can't be devoted to everything"
